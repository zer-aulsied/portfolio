About Me


My name is Justine, and I am a dedicated IT student with extensive experience in the video game industry. I have contributed to the development of prominent titles such as Valorant, Inside, and Arena of Valor.


My expertise includes core service architecture, progression systems, and large-scale game development, alongside strong leadership skills in managing and mentoring engineering teams. As both a student and a professional, I am committed to driving innovation and excellence to create immersive, high-quality gaming experiences.


I continuously seek to expand my knowledge of emerging technologies and apply them to advance both game development and software engineering practices.
